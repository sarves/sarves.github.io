Teaching philosophy
======
My aim is to develop lifelong learners, skilled collaborators, and innovative creators, whilst nurturing empathy and a sense of community contribution. My teaching, enriched by my own learning and extracurricular experiences, incorporates online learning, group work, social projects, guest lectures, and practical demonstrations. With years of industry experience, I employ a practical approach to teaching Computer Science, utilising real-world examples and industry insights. I promote self-learning and use a Learning Management System for material distribution, assignments, and examinations. I focus on building student confidence, encouraging participation, and rewarding engagement in discussions. My classes emphasise 21st-century skills, cultivated through group projects and practical problem-solving, and encourage extra-curricular involvement for comprehensive development. Additionally, I instil values of empathy and community service, urging students to actively contribute to society. Continuously adapting my teaching based on student feedback ensures an enhanced learning experience.

@Department of Computer Science, University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka.
